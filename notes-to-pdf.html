Turn messy notes into a clean PDF

Notes pile up across apps — Apple Notes, Google Keep, Notion, a phone memo, the back of an email. When you need to submit, print, or archive them, a PDF is the format that holds its shape everywhere. VECTOR converts notes to PDF right in your browser: paste your notes above and download a tidy, paginated document. No upload means your private notes never touch a server.

How to convert notes to PDF

  1. Copy your notes from any app, or type them straight into the box.
  2. Paste them into the workspace above.
  3. Press Download PDF and save your document.

Great for students and teams

Students convert lecture notes into PDFs to submit or revise from; teams turn meeting notes into shareable minutes; researchers archive findings in a format that will still open in ten years. Long note sets are split across pages automatically, each with a footer and page number, so a semester of notes becomes one clean file.
