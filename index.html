The InstrumentOnline Text to PDF Converter

Vector turns plain text into a clean, paginated PDF document without leaving the page. There is no upload step and no waiting room: the moment you press Download PDF, the file is composed in your browser and saved straight to your device. It is built for writers, developers, students, and anyone who needs a quick, private way to convert text to PDF.

How to convert text to PDF

  1. Type or paste your text into the workspace above.
  2. Press Download PDF.
  3. Your paginated PDF is generated locally and downloaded instantly.

Key features

  • 100% private — conversion runs entirely in your browser; your text never touches a server.
  • Automatic pagination — long documents flow across multiple pages with page numbers and a footer.
  • No install, no account — works on any modern desktop or mobile browser.
  • Free, forever — no watermark, no limits, no sign-up.
  • Draft memory — your last text is remembered on this device so nothing is lost.

Privacy & security

We do not store your data. All processing happens in-browser using client-side JavaScript and nothing is transmitted to us — your text is discarded the instant you close the tab. Read the full privacy notice and terms.
