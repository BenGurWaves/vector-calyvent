Why convert a text file to PDF?

PDFs are ideal when you need something to print cleanly, attach to an email, or archive without worrying about line-wrapping or font changes. Developers export logs and config dumps to PDF for tickets; students turn raw notes into submittable documents; writers convert drafts into a fixed format for review. Because VECTOR paginates automatically, even a very long text file becomes a tidy multi-page PDF with page numbers.
