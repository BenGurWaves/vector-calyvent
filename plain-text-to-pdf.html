Convert plain text to a fixed PDF document

Plain text has no styling — no fonts, sizes, or layout — which makes it flexible but unreliable to share, because it reflows differently in every app. Converting plain text to PDF locks it into a fixed, paginated page that renders identically everywhere and prints cleanly. VECTOR does this in your browser: paste your text above, press one button, and download the PDF. Nothing is uploaded.

How to convert plain text to PDF

  1. Type or paste your plain text into the box above.
  2. Press Download PDF.
  3. Your paginated PDF is generated locally and downloads instantly.

When you need it

Plain-text-to-PDF is handy for turning a README into a deliverable, attaching a log to a support ticket, archiving an email body, or printing a snippet without an editor mangling the spacing. The fixed layout is the advantage: what you see is exactly what prints.
