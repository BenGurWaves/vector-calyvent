Paste text and get a PDF in seconds

Sometimes you do not have a file — you just have text you copied from an email, a chat, a webpage, or a document, and you need it as a PDF. VECTOR is built for exactly that moment: paste the text into the box above and download a clean, paginated PDF immediately. There is no file to upload and no account to create, and because everything runs in your browser, whatever you paste never leaves your device.

How to paste text and save as PDF

  1. Select and copy text from any app (Ctrl/Cmd + C).
  2. Click the box above and paste (Ctrl/Cmd + V).
  3. Press Download PDF to save your document.

Example

Clipboard ->  "Action items:
               1. Ship the landing pages
               2. Submit sitemap"

PDF       ->  A4 document, fixed layout,
              VECTOR footer + page numbers.

Common uses

People paste text to PDF to save a receipt or confirmation that only exists as on-screen text, to archive a chat transcript, to hand off copy for print, or to turn a wall of notes into something they can email. The output is identical on every device, which is the whole point of a PDF.

Privacy & security

We do not store your data. All processing happens in-browser and your pasted text is never transmitted. See our privacy notice and terms.

FAQ

Can I paste from anywhere?
Yes — any app or webpage that lets you copy text.

Is it private?
Completely. Nothing is uploaded or stored.

Is there a length limit?
No — long text is paginated automatically.
